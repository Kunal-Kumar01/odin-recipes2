Lassi recipe

Description

Indian cold drink to beat the summer, blend of sweet and sour.

Ingredients

  • Ice cubes
  • 1 3/4 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 cups ice water
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Directions

  1. Fill 6 tall glasses with ice cubes. Place yogurt, ice water, crushed ice, sugar, and salt in a blender; blend until frothy. Pour over ice cubes in the glasses to serve.

Tip

  • You can substitute 1/2 teaspoon salt for sugar for a savory lassi.
